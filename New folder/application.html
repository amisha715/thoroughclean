Success Stories – Innovative ideas for your advantage

With more than 70 years of experience in the cleaning technology, SBS Ecoclean Group, formerly known as Dürr Ecoclean, builds technical cleaning applications and water-based surface treatment processes. A wide audience from the industrial market right from the automotive to manufacturers for medical and optical equipment appreciate our technology and our services.


Our state-of-the-art machines cater to a wide variety of industrial parts cleaning applications across the industrial markets in India and all over the world. These machines are currently used to clean components for the manufacturers from Automotive & E-Mobility, Non-Metallic, Aerospace, Railways, Metal Working, Precision Optics industries.
